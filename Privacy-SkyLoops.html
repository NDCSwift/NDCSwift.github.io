Introduction

Noah Carpenter ("we", "us", or "our") operates the Sky Loops mobile application (the "App"). This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our App.

Information We Collect

Automatically Collected Information

When you use Sky Loops, we automatically collect certain information through third-party services:

AdMob (Google)

  • Device identifiers (Advertising ID)
  • Usage data (ad impressions, clicks)
  • Approximate location (for ad targeting)

Game Center (Apple)

  • Leaderboard data
  • Achievement data
  • Player alias

Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do NOT collect your name, email, or contact information
  • We do NOT collect precise location data
  • We do NOT collect financial information
  • We do NOT create user accounts
  • We do NOT access your photos, contacts, or other personal files

How We Use Your Information

AdMob Data:

  • Display personalized advertisements
  • Measure ad performance
  • Improve ad relevance

Game Center Data:

  • Display leaderboards
  • Track achievements
  • Enable social features

Gameplay Data (stored locally on your device):

  • Save your progress
  • Track high scores
  • Manage unlocked items
  • Sync via iCloud (optional)

Third-Party Services

Sky Loops uses the following third-party services:

Google AdMob

  • Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy
  • Opt-out: https://adssettings.google.com

Apple Game Center

  • Privacy Policy: https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/

Data Retention

  • AdMob data: Managed by Google (see their privacy policy)
  • Game progress: Stored on your device indefinitely
  • You can delete all data by deleting the app

Children's Privacy

Sky Loops is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data (gameplay data is stored on your device)
  • Delete your data (by deleting the app)
  • Opt-out of personalized ads (via device settings)

To opt-out of personalized ads:

  • iOS: Settings → Privacy → Tracking → Disable "Allow Apps to Request to Track"

In-App Purchases

Sky Loops offers in-app purchases for virtual currency (coins). All purchases are processed by Apple. We do not collect or store payment information.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
