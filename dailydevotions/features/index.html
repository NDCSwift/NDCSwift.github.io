✝ Daily Devotions

One quiet moment. Every day.

Daily Devotions pulls from a hand-curated library of over 3,000 devotions, each paired with a scripture reference across four full Bible translations — all stored inside the app, so nothing needs to load or download later.

Translations

Four full Bible translations

KJV, ASV, WEB, and DRB — switch between them on any devotion, instantly and entirely offline.

Search

Full-text archive search

Find any devotion by keyword or scripture reference across the entire 3,000+ entry library.

Streaks

Streaks with real milestones

Reading streaks celebrate at 3, 7, 14, 30, 60, 100, and 365 days — a small, honest nudge to keep going.

Reflections

Private, written reflections

Add your own notes to any devotion. They're keyed to the date and stored only on your device.

Widget

Home Screen widget

Today's devotion sits on your Home Screen and refreshes automatically at midnight.

Sharing

Verse share cards

Turn any verse into a shareable image card — no screenshotting and cropping required.

Everything else, built in

The app is 100% free — nothing here is gated behind a purchase.

⭐️

Favorites

Save any devotion for later and build your own personal collection over time.

🔔

Optional daily reminders

Notifications are only requested the first time you mark a devotion as read — never at launch.

🌙

Dark mode

A full dark theme for reading at night, matching your system appearance or set manually.

Every devotion and translation ships inside the app — the app never needs a network connection to work.
