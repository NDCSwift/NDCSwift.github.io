What Daily Devotions is

Daily Devotions is a scripture and reflection app for iPhone and iPad that works completely offline. Every devotion — over 3,000 of them, each with a scripture reference across four full Bible translations (KJV, ASV, WEB, DRB) — ships inside the app as a local library, so there's no loading spinner and nothing to download later. You can search the full archive, save favorites, write private reflections, build a reading streak with milestone celebrations, and keep a home screen widget going — all without an account or an internet connection.
