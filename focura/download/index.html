Download Focura

Focura is free to download on the App Store. The core timer, Flow State, calendar mirroring, widgets, and Live Activities are free; a Premium subscription unlocks unlimited recurring blocks, Smart Reschedule, and full session history.

Download on the App Store →

Requirements

  • iPhone running iOS (iPad compatible — runs the iPhone layout)
  • No account or sign-up required
  • Optional permissions: Calendar (read-only), Reminders (read-only), Notifications

Pricing

  • Free tier: full timer + up to 7 recurring time blocks (one-off blocks unlimited)
  • Premium: $4.99/month · $19.99/year · $99.99 lifetime (USD; regional prices vary)

Subscriptions auto-renew unless cancelled at least 24 hours before the end of the current period. Manage or cancel anytime in Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions.
