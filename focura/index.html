Focura is an iOS time-blocking application that helps adults with ADHD plan their day. It combines an adaptive focus timer, a visual timeline, read-only iOS Calendar and Reminders integration, Live Activities, and Home Screen widgets. All data is stored on-device, and the app requires no account.

Free to use · iPhone (iPad compatible) · Premium optional

Built around one question

Most focus timers ship one rigid setup. Focura asks whether you struggle to start, stay, or stop — then tunes the timer length, transition buffers, and haptics to match. Built for ADHD brains; works for anyone fighting a 25-minute box.

🌊 Flow State, not overtime

Run past the timer and the screen turns green. Hyperfocus is celebrated, not punished with alarms.

📅 Your calendar becomes the plan

iOS Calendar events appear as locked time blocks. A meeting starting mid-session auto-pauses your timer.

🔄 Aggressively forgiving

Missed a block? Smart Reschedule finds it a new slot. Streaks include freeze days. Fresh Start resets the day without erasing it.

🔒 100% on-device

No account, no cloud, no tracking. Your blocks and sessions live in your iPhone's local database.
