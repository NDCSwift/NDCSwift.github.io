Focura Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Focura is built for people who want to focus better. Your data is yours — Focura stores everything locally on your device and does not sell or share your personal information.

What We Collect

Focura collects no personal information. All data — time blocks, sessions, streaks, and settings — is stored locally on your device using SQLite and never leaves it.

Calendar and Reminders Access

If you grant permission, Focura reads your iOS Calendar events and Reminders to display them in your timeline. This data is read-only, processed on-device, and never transmitted to any server.

Analytics

Focura uses PostHog for optional, privacy-respecting analytics. No personally identifiable information is collected. Analytics help us understand which features are useful and fix bugs.

Purchases

In-app purchases are processed by Apple through the App Store. Focura uses RevenueCat to manage subscription status. RevenueCat receives an anonymous user identifier and purchase receipt — no name, email, or other personal data.

Notifications

If enabled, Focura sends local notifications for timer completions and daily reminders. These are generated on-device and are not transmitted anywhere.

Data Storage

All app data is stored locally on your device. Deleting the app removes all data permanently. There is no cloud backup or account system.

Children

Focura is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes

We may update this policy as the app evolves. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
