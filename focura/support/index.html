Focura Support

Focura is built by one indie developer — Noah Carpenter (NDC). Questions, bug reports, and feature requests all land in the same inbox and get read: noahdoescoding@gmail.com

Does the timer keep running in the background?

Yes. Focura anchors every session to the wall clock and schedules a local notification for the session's end, so the timer stays accurate even if you close the app or lock the phone. No background tracking is involved.

Where is my data stored?

All blocks and sessions live in a local database on your iPhone. There is no account, no cloud sync, and no tracking. Deleting the app deletes the data.

How do I restore a Premium purchase?

Open Settings inside Focura and tap Restore Purchases. Purchases are tied to your Apple ID.

How do I cancel the subscription?

Subscriptions are managed by Apple: iOS Settings → your Apple ID → Subscriptions → Focura. Cancelling at least 24 hours before the renewal date prevents the next charge.

Is Focura a medical device?

No. Focura is a tool, not a treatment. It is not a medical device and is not a substitute for professional care.
