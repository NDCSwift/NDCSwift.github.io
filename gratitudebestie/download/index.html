Requirements

Compatibility

Gratitude Bestie runs on iPhone and iPad (iOS/iPadOS 18 or later), and is also available on Mac with Apple silicon (macOS 15+) and Apple Vision Pro (visionOS 2.0+). The free version shows a small banner ad; a one-time $0.99 purchase removes it. iCloud sync is optional and uses your own private iCloud account.
