💛 Gratitude Bestie
Overview Features Download Support

Notice it. Name it. Keep it.

Gratitude Bestie is built around three moves: capture a moment quickly (type, talk, or snap a photo), understand how it feels without extra effort, and revisit it later in a way that actually feels good.

Capture

Type, dictate, or snap a photo

Write an entry, use voice dictation instead, and attach up to six photos from your library or the camera.

Emotion Tagging

An on-device emotion tag, automatically

A Core ML model reads each entry's text on your device and suggests a tag from 15 categories — Gratitude, Joy, Peace/Calm, Pride, and more.

Trends

Charts that show your patterns

Entries per day, time-of-day distribution, top tags, and a donut chart of your top emotions — plus your current streak.

Memory Lane

Curated moments worth revisiting

On this day, last week, last month, and all-time highlights, automatically curated from your journal — with a bit of pleasant surprise mixed in.

Sharing

Shareable entry cards

Turn any entry — text and photo — into a card ready to send or post, no screenshotting required.

Theming

Six color themes

System, Pink, Orange, Lilac, Teal, or Mint — pick the palette that matches your mood.

Everything else, built in

Your journal syncs privately to your own iCloud account — never to a server we run.

⭐️

Favorites & tags

Mark entries as favorites and organize them with your own custom tags for quick filtering later.

🔔

Daily reminder

An optional local notification, defaulting to 8pm, reminds you to jot down today's good thing.

☁️

iCloud sync

Entries, photos, and tags sync across your devices through your own private iCloud account.

🔍

Searchable archive

Browse every past entry, filter by tag or emotion, and pick up right where you left off.

📶

Works without a connection

Writing, tagging, and browsing all work fully offline — only iCloud sync needs a connection.

💸

Optional ad-free unlock

The app is free with a small banner ad. A one-time $0.99 purchase removes it — restorable on any device.
