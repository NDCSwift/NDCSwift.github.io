What Gratitude Bestie is

Gratitude Bestie is a gratitude journal for iPhone and iPad built around noticing one good thing a day. Entries can be typed or dictated by voice, paired with up to six photos, and organized with your own tags. An on-device Core ML model suggests an emotion tag for each entry from a 15-category taxonomy, and a Trends screen turns your entries into charts — entries per day, time-of-day patterns, top tags, and your emotional mix over time. Memory Lane resurfaces past entries — on this day, last week, last month, or your all-time highlights — and any entry can be shared as a photo card. The app is free, with a small banner ad shown unless you make the optional one-time purchase to remove it, and it works with iCloud to sync your journal across your own devices.
