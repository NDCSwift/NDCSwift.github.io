Gratitude Bestie's free version displays banner advertisements served by Google's AdMob. Ads are subject to Google's own terms and content policies, which are outside our control. You may remove ads entirely with the optional purchase described below.

Purchases

Gratitude Bestie offers one optional in-app purchase, processed through Apple's App Store:

  • Disable Ads ($0.99, one-time) — removes all in-app advertising. It is a non-consumable purchase, is not a subscription, and never renews or auto-charges.
  • As a non-consumable, this purchase can be restored at any time via "Restore Purchases" on any device signed into the same Apple ID.
  • The rest of the app — journaling, photos, emotion tagging, Trends, Memory Lane, streaks, and themes — is free and not gated behind any purchase.
  • Refunds are handled by Apple in accordance with their refund policy.

Emotion Tagging

Emotion tags are generated automatically by an on-device model as a suggestion based on your entry's text. They are provided for personal reflection only and are not a psychological or clinical assessment.
