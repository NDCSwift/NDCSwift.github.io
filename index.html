About Me

I started in web design — HTML, CSS, JavaScript — then taught myself Swift because I wanted to build something that lived in someone's pocket. That was five years ago. Since then I've shipped 6 apps across games, productivity, wellness, and most recently my first cross-platform app with React Native.

App development is genuinely hard — especially when you're learning on your own. That's the reason I make tutorials. My YouTube channel covers concepts I've had to work through myself, explained thoroughly enough that you actually understand what's happening — not just what to type. Every video comes with a forkable GitHub repo. I'd rather over-explain than leave someone stuck where I once was.

Philosophy
