About Me

I'm Noah Carpenter, an indie iOS developer and educator with 5+ years of experience shipping apps on the App Store. I started with a formal background in web design, taught myself Swift and SwiftUI, and have since expanded into React Native for cross-platform work.

My focus is on building apps people genuinely enjoy using — with clear design, real accessibility, and a goal of helping other developers grow along the way. Every public repo on my GitHub is a tutorial companion, and I cover everything from SwiftUI fundamentals to on-device AI on my YouTube channel.
