RealRate Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

RealRate is built to help you understand your side hustle income. Your financial data is yours — RealRate stores everything on your device and in your own iCloud account. We do not sell or share your personal information.

What We Collect

RealRate collects no personal information. All data — gigs, sessions, expenses, and settings — is stored locally on your device using SwiftData and never transmitted to our servers.

iCloud Sync

If you have iCloud enabled, your RealRate data syncs across your devices via Apple's CloudKit. This data is stored in your personal iCloud account and is governed by Apple's Privacy Policy. We have no access to it.

Purchases

In-app purchases are processed by Apple through the App Store. RealRate uses RevenueCat to manage subscription entitlements. RevenueCat receives an anonymous user identifier and purchase receipt — no name, email, or other personal data is shared.

Notifications

If you grant permission, RealRate sends local notifications to remind you to log your sessions. These are generated on-device and are not transmitted anywhere.

Analytics

RealRate does not use any third-party analytics or tracking SDKs. No usage data is collected or transmitted.

Data Storage and Deletion

All app data is stored locally on your device and, if enabled, in your personal iCloud account. Deleting the app removes local data permanently. To remove iCloud data, delete it from iCloud Drive in your device settings.

Children

RealRate is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes

We may update this policy as the app evolves. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
