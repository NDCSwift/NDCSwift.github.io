Compatibility

Sky Loops runs on iPhone and iPad (iOS/iPadOS 26 or later), and is also available on Mac with Apple silicon (macOS 26+) and Apple Vision Pro (visionOS 26+). No account is required to play — Game Center is only needed for leaderboards and some achievements.
