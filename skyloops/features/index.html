Tap. Loop. Chain the sky.

Every mode in Sky Loops runs on the same core loop — steer through a ring, land it clean, and watch your combo climb — but each one tests it a little differently.

Zen Mode

Endless flight, zero pressure

No timer, no death. Fly as long as you like — idle at the bottom of the screen for 5 seconds to end the run.

60-Second Challenge

Quick combo sprint

Land the biggest combo you can in a single minute, then check the leaderboard.

120-Second Challenge

Room to build a real streak

Twice the time to find your rhythm and stack a serious combo multiplier.

180-Second Challenge

The long run

Three minutes to prove your combo game holds up under sustained pressure.

Combo Scoring

Perfect rings pay more

Every ring earns coins; a perfectly-timed pass earns a bonus, and your peak combo adds even more at the end of a run.

Tutorial

Learn the timing safely

A guided tutorial run with death disabled, so you can learn the ring timing before it counts.

Cosmetics worth earning

11 planes and 8 trails, unlocked with coins earned from actually playing — no pay-to-win.
