What Sky Loops is

Sky Loops is a paper plane arcade game for iPhone and iPad built around one tap-timed core loop: steer through a ring, land it perfectly, and keep your combo multiplier climbing. Zen Mode offers endless, pressure-free flight, while 60/120/180-second timed challenges turn the same mechanic into a race for the biggest combo before time runs out. Coins earned from every run unlock 11 planes and 8 trails across five rarity tiers — Starter, Common, Rare, Epic, and Legendary — alongside daily challenges, 20 achievements, Game Center leaderboards, and iCloud sync across your devices.
