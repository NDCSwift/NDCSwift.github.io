Sky Loops Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Sky Loops is a paper plane arcade game. There is no account or sign-up, and your gameplay data — coins, unlocked planes and trails, achievement progress — stays on your device and, if you enable it, in your own private iCloud account. This app shows advertising and involves some data collection through that advertising, described in full below. We do not sell your personal information.

Gameplay Data

Coins, unlocked cosmetics, high scores, and daily challenge progress are stored locally on your device and, if iCloud is enabled, synced through Apple's CloudKit to your own private iCloud account. We have no server of our own and no access to this data.

Advertising and Tracking

Sky Loops shows banner, interstitial, and optional rewarded ads through Google's AdMob unless you purchase the one-time "Remove Ads" upgrade (banner and interstitial ads only — rewarded videos remain available even after this purchase, since they're optional). To support this, the app:

  • Requests permission via Apple's App Tracking Transparency (ATT) framework before allowing tracking-based ad personalization. You can decline this — the app and its ads still work, just without personalization.
  • Shares device identifiers and usage data with Google/AdMob for ad serving, measurement, and personalization, and may share this data with third-party advertising and analytics partners. Per Apple's App Privacy disclosures, some of this data is used to track you across other companies' apps and websites when tracking is authorized.
  • Does not share your gameplay progress, coins, or unlocked cosmetics with any advertising partner — advertising data collection is limited to ad-serving mechanics.

Google's use of this data is governed by Google's Privacy Policy. You can review or withdraw the ATT permission anytime in your device's Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking, and you can opt out of personalized advertising generally at adssettings.google.com.

Game Center

If you sign in to Game Center, Sky Loops submits your scores to per-mode leaderboards and syncs some achievements. This is handled entirely by Apple and governed by Apple's Privacy Policy. Game Center is optional — every game mode works without it.

Purchases

Coin packs and the Remove Ads upgrade are processed entirely by Apple through the App Store using StoreKit. We do not use any third-party payment SDK, so no purchase data is shared with anyone but Apple.

Data Storage and Deletion

Gameplay data lives on your device and, if enabled, your personal iCloud account. Deleting the app removes local data. Leaderboard scores submitted to Game Center are managed through your Apple ID.

Children

Sky Loops is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes

We may update this policy as the app evolves. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
