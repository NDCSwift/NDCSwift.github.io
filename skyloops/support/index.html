Support

Answers to the questions I get most often. Can't find yours? Email me directly below.

Does Sky Loops show ads, and does it track me?

Yes — the free version shows banner, interstitial, and optional rewarded ads via Google AdMob, and asks for App Tracking Transparency (ATT) permission to personalize them. Declining ATT still lets you play fully; ads just won't be personalized.

How do I earn coins?

Coins come from passing rings (more for a perfectly-timed pass), your peak combo at the end of a run, a first-win-of-the-day bonus, and optional rewarded ads (up to 3 a day). Coin packs are also available for purchase.

Can I restore my Remove Ads purchase on a new device?

Yes. Remove Ads is a one-time, non-consumable purchase, restorable anytime via "Restore Purchases" on any device signed into the same Apple ID. Rewarded videos stay available even after removing ads, since they're optional and give you coins.

Do I need Game Center to play?

No — every mode works without signing in. Game Center is only used for per-mode leaderboards and syncing some achievements.

Does my progress sync across devices?

Yes, if you're signed into iCloud. Coins, unlocked planes and trails, and progress sync automatically through your own private iCloud account.

How often do daily challenges reset?

Daily challenges refresh once every day, each with its own coin reward for completing it.

Still stuck?

Email me directly and I'll get back to you as soon as I can — usually within a couple of days. Purchases and refunds are handled by Apple, but I'm happy to point you the right way.
