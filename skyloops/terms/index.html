Purchases

Sky Loops offers optional in-app purchases processed through Apple's App Store:

  • Starter Pack ($0.99), Popular Pack ($2.99), Mega Pack ($4.99), and Ultimate Pack ($9.99) — consumable coin packs, spent on in-game cosmetics.
  • Remove Ads ($2.99, one-time) — removes banner and interstitial ads. It is a non-consumable purchase, is not a subscription, and never renews.
  • Remove Ads can be restored at any time via "Restore Purchases" on any device signed into the same Apple ID. Coin packs are consumables and are not restorable once spent.
  • No purchase is required to unlock any plane or trail — every cosmetic is earnable through regular gameplay.
  • Refunds are handled by Apple in accordance with their refund policy.
