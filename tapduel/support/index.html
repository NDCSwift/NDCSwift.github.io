Support

Answers to the questions I get most often. Can't find yours? Email me directly below.

How does Tap Duel measure my reaction time?

Tap Duel times the interval, on-device, from the moment the circle turns green to the moment you tap it, measured to the millisecond. No network round-trip is involved, so the measurement reflects your actual tap speed, not connection latency.

What's a good reaction time in Tap Duel?

On a touchscreen, anything under roughly 300ms is solid — touch input typically runs a little slower than a mouse click. The app shows your live percentile against other players so you can judge your score in context instead of guessing.

Do I need Game Center to play?

No — Reflex Mode, Daily Challenge, Frenzy, Challenge, and Local Duel all work without signing in. Game Center is only required for online 1v1 matchmaking and to appear on global leaderboards.

What does Tap Duel Pro unlock?

Tap Duel Pro is a one-time $4.99 purchase that unlocks every premium app icon pack and color at once. It never renews and is not a subscription.

How do I request a refund?

All purchases are processed by Apple through the App Store, so refunds are handled by Apple directly at reportaproblem.apple.com.

Does Tap Duel work offline?

Yes — Reflex Mode, Daily Challenge, Frenzy, Challenge, and Local Duel all work fully offline. Only online 1v1 duels and Game Center leaderboards require an internet connection.

Still stuck?

Email me directly and I'll get back to you as soon as I can — usually within a couple of days. Purchases and refunds are handled by Apple, but I'm happy to point you the right way.
