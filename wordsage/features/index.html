Four modes. One vocabulary.

WordSage combines four distinct practice modes, all drawing from the same hand-curated library of 1,000+ words, so review never feels repetitive. Every entry — definition, synonyms, hints, and example sentence — was written by hand, not generated.

Daily Challenge

Guess of the day

One word, progressively revealing hints. Guess it to keep your streak alive — miss it and spend a Literacy Lifeline to protect the streak instead.

Word Scramble

Beat the anagram

Unscramble letters against the clock. Chain correct guesses fast enough to trigger Fever mode and multiply your score up to 5x.

Flashcards

Spaced repetition

Swipe right when you know it, left to review again. A proper SM-2 scheduler decides when each word comes back.

Memory Match

Flip and match

Find every word pair before you run out of guesses. Clear the board to earn a Literacy Lifeline for your next session.

Everything else, built in

Progress, purchases, and streaks all stay on your own devices — nothing is collected behind the scenes.

📚

1,000+ handpicked words

Every entry — definition, synonyms, hints, example sentences — was chosen and written by hand, not generated.

🔥

Streaks worth protecting

Miss a day and you can spend a Literacy Lifeline to protect your streak instead of losing it outright.

☁️

iCloud sync

Progress, stats, and known words sync across your iPhone and iPad via CloudKit — private to your own iCloud account.

✨

Haptics in every interaction

Correct guesses, wrong guesses, purchases, and streak saves each have their own considered haptic feedback.

💡

Literacy Lifelines

An optional in-app currency used to reveal hints or protect a streak — 10 for a one-time $0.99, never a subscription.

🔒

No accounts, no tracking

No sign-up, no third-party analytics or ad SDKs. What you play stays between you and your own device.
