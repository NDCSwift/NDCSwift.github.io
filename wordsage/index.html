What WordSage is

WordSage is a vocabulary game for iPhone and iPad built around active recall instead of passive flashcard swiping. It combines four practice modes — a daily hint-based challenge, a timed anagram scramble, spaced-repetition flashcards, and a memory-matching game — pulling from a hand-curated library of over 1,000 words. Progress and streaks sync privately across devices through iCloud, and the app requires no account and collects no personal data.
