Daily Challenge

Guess of the day

One word, progressively revealing hints. Guess it to keep your streak alive — miss it and spend a Lifeline to protect the streak instead.

Word Scramble

Beat the anagram

Unscramble letters against the clock. Chain correct guesses fast enough to trigger Fever mode and multiply your score up to 5x.

Flashcards

Spaced repetition

Memory Match

Flip and match

Find every word pair before you run out of guesses. Clear the board to earn a Lifeline for your next session.
