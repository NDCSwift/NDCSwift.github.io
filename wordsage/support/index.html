Support

Answers to the questions I get most often. Can't find yours? Email me directly below.

How do I sync my progress between iPhone and iPad?

Enable iCloud on both devices and make sure WordSage has iCloud access in Settings → Apple ID → iCloud. Progress, streaks, and known words sync automatically through Apple's CloudKit.

What are Literacy Lifelines?

Lifelines are an optional in-app currency used to reveal hints or protect a streak you're about to lose. You can buy a pack of 10 for $0.99 — a one-time purchase, not a subscription.

I missed a day and lost my streak — can I get it back?

Spend a Literacy Lifeline before midnight to protect a streak that's about to break. Once a streak resets, it can't be restored afterward.

How do I request a refund?

All purchases are processed by Apple through the App Store, so refunds are handled by Apple directly at reportaproblem.apple.com.

Does WordSage work offline?

Yes. All word content ships inside the app, so every mode works without an internet connection. iCloud sync happens automatically whenever you're back online.

How do I turn off the daily reminder?

Open Settings on your device, find WordSage, and turn off notifications — or disable the reminder directly inside the app's settings screen.

Still stuck?

Email me directly and I'll get back to you as soon as I can — usually within a couple of days. Purchases and refunds are handled by Apple, but I'm happy to point you the right way.
